### Ranked List of Likely Public Concerns

1. **Land Use and Planning**
   - **Concern:** Conversion of active agricultural land to residential use.
   - **Rationale:** The project proposes to develop on currently active orchard land, which could raise concerns about the loss of agricultural resources and impacts on local food production.

2. **Biological Resources**
   - **Concern:** Potential impacts on local wildlife and habitats.
   - **Rationale:** The development of residential units on orchard land may disrupt existing ecosystems and wildlife habitats, leading to concerns about biodiversity loss and habitat fragmentation.

3. **Hydrology and Water Quality**
   - **Concern:** Management of stormwater runoff from the new development.
   - **Rationale:** The inclusion of a storm basin indicates potential concerns about how stormwater will be managed, especially given the increase in impervious surfaces from residential construction, which could affect local water quality.

4. **Transportation**
   - **Concern:** Increased traffic congestion and safety issues.
   - **Rationale:** The addition of up to 615 residential units will likely generate significant traffic, raising concerns about road capacity, safety for pedestrians and cyclists, and overall traffic management in the area.

5. **Population and Housing**
   - **Concern:** Strain on local infrastructure and services.
   - **Rationale:** The influx of new residents may place additional demands on local services such as schools, emergency services, and utilities, leading to concerns about whether existing infrastructure can accommodate the growth.

6. **Air Quality**
   - **Concern:** Increased air pollution from construction and residential activities.
   - **Rationale:** Construction activities and increased vehicle emissions from new residents could contribute to air quality degradation, raising concerns among community members about health impacts.

7. **Noise**
   - **Concern:** Noise pollution from construction and increased residential activity.
   - **Rationale:** The construction phase and subsequent residential use could generate significant noise, which may disturb nearby residents and wildlife, prompting public concern about quality of life and environmental impacts.
