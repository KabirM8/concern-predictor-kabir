# Centennial Specific Plan Project

**SCH:** 2024031142
**Lead Agency:** Los Angeles County
**Location:** Unincorporated Los Angeles County, near SR-138/Gorman Post Road
**Document Type:** NOP (Notice of Preparation of Supplemental EIR)

## Project Description

The applicant proposes minor modifications to a previously approved 2019 master-planned community. Key changes include incorporating utility-scale battery storage and microgrids to enhance renewable energy resilience and support Net Zero GHG objectives, and revising internal roadway design standards to increase evacuation capacity.

The overall project scope remains a master-planned community comprising 19,333 residential units along with office, commercial, educational, and recreational facilities across 12,323 acres. The site is located in a rural, largely undeveloped area of northern Los Angeles County near the Tehachapi Mountains.

A Supplemental EIR will analyze greenhouse gas emissions and wildfire impacts per the Climate Resolve settlement, while assessing consistency with County General Plan 2035 and the Antelope Valley Area Plan.
