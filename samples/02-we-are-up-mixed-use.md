# We Are Up Mixed-Use Planned Development Project

**SCH:** 2024110559
**Lead Agency:** Humboldt County
**Location:** 1515 Central Avenue, McKinleyville, unincorporated Humboldt County
**Document Type:** NOP (Notice of Preparation of Draft EIR)

## Project Description

The project proposes a new infill Mixed-Use Planned Development consisting of up to 70 residential units for seniors and individuals with disabilities, a four-story Mixed-Use Community Center, a greenhouse, garden space, orchard, recreation facilities, and walking trails.

The community center will accommodate residential uses, retail operations, and temporary programming including classes, trainings, meetings, community meals, and weddings. The site encompasses approximately 17.58 acres across four parcels.

The project would fill 13,470 square feet of existing wetlands while mitigating losses through converting 16,052 square feet of upland areas into new wetlands. Water and sewer services will connect to the McKinleyville Community Services District. The project also includes a lot line adjustment of the four parcels to facilitate development.
