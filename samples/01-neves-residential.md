# Neves Residential Project

**SCH:** 2024120645
**Lead Agency:** City of Hanford
**Location:** Northwest corner of Fargo and 12th Avenues, Hanford, Kings County
**Document Type:** NOP (Notice of Preparation of Draft EIR)

## Project Description

The applicant proposes constructing up to 615 single-family residential units across approximately 135.28 acres of currently active orchard land. The development includes a 5.87-acre storm basin and a seven-acre park along with access roads, lighting, landscaping and other associated improvements per City Standards.

The project requires a Tentative Subdivision Map and Planned Unit Development overlay to enable smaller lot sizes and reduced setbacks. Development would occur in four phases: Phase I consists of 140 units on 44.63 acres plus the 7-acre park and storm basin; Phase II includes 229 units on 44.49 acres; Phase III includes 185 units on 34.57 acres; Phase IV includes 57 units on 11.59 acres.

The site is currently in active agricultural use (orchard).
