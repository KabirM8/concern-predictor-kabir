# Wildcat Energy Farm Project

**SCH:** 2024110680
**Lead Agency:** Imperial County
**Location:** Near Salton City, Imperial County (approximately 5,900 acres of privately-owned land near SR 86)
**Document Type:** NOP (Notice of Preparation of Draft EIR)

## Project Description

The applicant proposes constructing and operating an 800 megawatt (MW) photovoltaic (PV) solar facility with an integrated 800 MW (3,200 MW hour) battery energy storage system (BESS). The facility would comprise roughly 1.96 million solar modules, collector and inverter infrastructure, a groundwater well, and access roads.

The project also involves upgrading the Imperial Irrigation District's transmission line infrastructure from 161 kilovolt to 230 kilovolt capacity and expanding the Wildcat Line to accommodate the same voltage level.

The site currently has mixed zoning including vacant/low-density residential, medium commercial, and open space designations. The project area is located near the Salton Sea in the Imperial Valley.
